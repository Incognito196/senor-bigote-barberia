Our Story

More Than a Barbershop.
A Brotherhood.

Since 2021, Senor Bigote Barberia has been the spot on Dyckman Street where gentlemen come for more than just a haircut. We built this shop to be a place where craft meets community — where every fade is precise, every shave is smooth, and every visit feels like coming home.

With a team of skilled barbers who take pride in their art, we've become a cornerstone of the neighborhood. Whether you need a clean fade, a straight-razor shave, or just a good conversation, we've got you.
